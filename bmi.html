BMI Calculator

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation that uses your weight and height to estimate body fat. It is widely used as a general indicator of whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.

How to use this BMI calculator?

Enter your weight in kilograms and your height in centimeters, then click calculate. The tool will instantly show your BMI result.
